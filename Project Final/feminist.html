Feminist

 

The feminist critical theory exemplifies the male dominance over the female counterpart in society. Gender roles became increasingly implemented throughout society usually depicting men as the successful creation of nature. On the other hand women were depicted as almost a fault in society. The dominance of the patriarchal society has prevailed throughout the ages and has presented itself throughout society. These concepts were revealed through literature in which the oppression of women was exemplified through political, social, economic, and psychological views.
